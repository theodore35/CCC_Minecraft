All players start off in the creative world at “The HUB”. Shoutout to Teddy_P for creating the spawn area and community signs. To switch to creative, simply right-click the sign and then use our set-home feature to teleport back to your build area

Survival

The majority of players end up in the CCC SMP, this survival mode is for soft-core or casual gamers. Using /spawn command you can warp to the worlds spawn (separate from the hub) and use the new “random tp” to quickly access a new area to build a base.

Hardcore

Unlike in normal HC, you will not be kicked from the world or have to restart. Major features of the CCC SMP HC are Modgriefing and Keep inventory is turned off. Enjoy a more rough and survival focused experience. Coming soon to HC are tougher mobs and zombie swarms!
